01 Solution Overview

Our solution is Shareflect, a website that allows groups of individuals to collect, organize, and share all types of multi-media with each other.

The goal of the Shareflect is to collect shared experiences for groups of people. It preserves memories for sharing and reflection. Further, it fosters feelings of community by allowing people to have meaningful dialogue about past events, thereby creating motivations to continue their friendship.

Finally, Shareflect allows users to export more than just their photos, but the meaningful exchanges that have transpired over the website as well.
