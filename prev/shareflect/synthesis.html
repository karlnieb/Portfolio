Synthesis

01 Synthesis Overview

From the research we conducted, we performed several activities which helped us synthesize all the things we learned. This synthesis involved the following activities:

  • Based on our interviews and directed storytelling we created a mood board to capture the elements we wanted our site to embody.
  • We created two personas to help our team maintain focus during design.
  • We constructed a model that shows both the process and values when collecting and sharing memories with a group of people.
