Summary

Shareflect is a website for groups who love to collect and share memories with each other.

Shareflect allows groups to collect, organize, share, and remember events involving their friends. It provides a community in which members can collect many types of multi-media and multiple perspectives from different events in order to preserve memories to reflect upon them later.

This process book outlines the design process we used to go from a blank slate to user research to a final solution.
